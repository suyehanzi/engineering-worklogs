# Sanitized Record Index

These files are public excerpts derived from a private daily worklog archive. They keep the useful engineering shape: goal, timeline, changes, evidence, problems, and next actions. Private names, full paths, credentials, internal links, device identifiers, screenshots, and customer details are removed or generalized.

## How To Use This Index

For a fast review, read the table first and then open one or two records that match the skill area you care about. Each record is intentionally more detailed than a resume bullet. The purpose is to show the working process behind a result:

- what the goal was
- what constraints existed
- how the task was broken down
- what was verified
- what risk was recorded
- what follow-up remained

For project-style examples that are not tied to one dated record, see [Public Case Studies](../docs/CASE-STUDIES.md).

## Public Boundary

- No original private archive is published.
- No passwords, keys, cookies, session values, private paths, or server details are included.
- No full customer names, contact details, payment records, or private account details are included.
- Records are rewritten to preserve workflow evidence without exposing private operational context.
